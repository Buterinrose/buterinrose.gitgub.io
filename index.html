About

A scarce amount of astonishing Buterin rosebuds has been sighted in a secret garden on Ethereum. It has been reported by botanists that this particular specie is a tribute to the founder of the network and that the beauty of the rose is influenced by the ethereum balance of the collector.

A group of florists are reputedly fascinated by the fact that the specie is entirely generated and stored on Ethereum with no external storage dependencies or behind-the-scenes randomization. The florists can read all data on-chain but what kind of creations do they want to use the roses for?

The secret garden is kept healthy by the Buterin specie. When all the Buterin roses has been collected, the garden will dry up. Imagine the difficulty of picking up a Buterin rose with a supreme appearance in a future with major network adoption and massive price appreciation of Ethereum.

FAQ

What is a Buterin rose?

The Buterin rose is a random generative art piece fully generated and stored on-chain. The Buterin rose takes form in the digital world as beautiful timeless animations stored as unique ERC-721 tokens.

What makes the Buterin rose special?

The Buterin rose is the first project where the art is influenced by the Ethereum balance of the minter and everything takes place on-chain. The collectibles are backed by strong chain security and there are no file storage dependencies nor behind-the-scenes randomization involved.

When will the Buterin rose be launched?

The Buterin rose collection will launch publicly on this website. Once sold out, then the collectibles will only be purchasable via secondary markets such as OpenSea.

How much will it cost to mint a Buterin rose?

The pricing model is based on a flat rate throughout the entire sale. The Buterin rose will launch at the price of 0.0580 ETH + gas costs.

How many Buterin roses can be minted?

The minting ceiling has been set to 10 Buterin roses per transaction. Buyers can execute as many transactions as desired until the total supply of tokens has been minted.

What happens to the secondary market royalties?

The Buterin rose encourages open market trading. The royalty percentage from secondary market sales has been set to 5% and a royalty split will be claimable and shared with the token holders based on the achieved roadmap milestones. Requirements: royalties will be shared once a month when a minimum of 1 ETH worth of royalties has been accumulated.
